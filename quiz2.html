PSLE Chinese Quiz

1. 在寒冷的冬天，人们一进到温暖的室内，马上便感到心情 ____________。





Correct answer: B 舒畅

“舒畅” means to feel comfortable, which fits the context of entering a warm room in winter.

2. 除了好奇心的 ____________，科学家的探索精神也使他们不断创新。





Correct answer: A 驱动

“驱动” means to drive or motivate, which is appropriate for describing how curiosity drives scientists.

3. 他本来计划去旅游，但突如其来的工作使他 ____________ 了出行计划。





Correct answer: A 取消

“取消” means to cancel, which fits the context of changing travel plans due to work.

4. 他的言行总是 ____________，从不考虑后果。





Correct answer: B 不假思索

“不假思索” means without thinking, which fits the context of acting without considering consequences.

5. 每次购物前， ____________ 先想想这个东西是否真的需要。





Correct answer: B 不妨

“不妨” means might as well, which fits the context of suggesting a reflective action before shopping.

6. 他的决定 ____________ 了团队的努力，大家都很失望。





Correct answer: D 否定

“否定” means to negate, which fits the context of negating the team's efforts.

7. 他做事总是 ____________，从来不会犹豫。





Correct answer: A 毫不犹豫

“毫不犹豫” means without hesitation, which fits the context of acting decisively.

8. 在紧急情况下，他 ____________ 地处理了问题。





Correct answer: B 不慌不忙

“不慌不忙” means calmly, which fits the context of handling an emergency calmly.

9. 他在关键时刻 ____________ 出了惊人的勇气。





Correct answer: D 发挥

“发挥” means to display or exert, which fits the context of showing courage at a critical moment.

10. 我们要 ____________ 对待每一次失败，从中吸取教训。





Correct answer: B 重视

“重视” means to value or take seriously, which fits the context of learning from failures.
