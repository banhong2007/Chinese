PSLE Chinese Quiz

1. 他在比赛前 ____________ ，希望能取得好成绩。





Correct answer: B 兴奋

“兴奋” means excited, which fits the context of feeling before a competition.

2. 他的成功 ____________ 于不懈的努力和坚持。





Correct answer: C 归功

“归功” means to attribute, which fits the context of attributing success to effort and persistence.

3. 他在演讲中 ____________ 了许多有趣的故事，赢得了观众的掌声。





Correct answer: A 讲述

“讲述” means to tell or narrate, which fits the context of telling interesting stories in a speech.

4. 他总是 ____________ 面对挑战，从不退缩。





Correct answer: A 勇敢

“勇敢” means brave, which fits the context of facing challenges without retreating.

5. ____________ 听到这个好消息，大家都欢呼起来。





Correct answer: D 一旦

“一旦” means once, which fits the context of everyone cheering upon hearing the good news.

6. 他的解释 ____________ 了我的疑惑。





Correct answer: B 解除了

“解除了” means to remove or resolve, which fits the context of resolving doubts.

7. 他对艺术有 ____________ 的兴趣，经常参加各种艺术展览。





Correct answer: B 浓厚

“浓厚” means strong or keen, which fits the context of having a keen interest in art.

8. 在困难面前，他从不 ____________，总是坚持到底。





Correct answer: A 放弃

“放弃” means to give up, which fits the context of never giving up in the face of difficulties.

9. 他的 ____________ 赢得了大家的尊重。





Correct answer: D 谦虚

“谦虚” means humility, which fits the context of winning respect through humility.

10. ____________ 他已经很努力了，但还是没有取得好成绩。





Correct answer: A 虽然

“虽然” means although, which fits the context of stating that despite his efforts, he did not achieve good results.

11. 他对这项任务表现出 ____________ 的责任感。





Correct answer: A 强烈

“强烈” means strong, which fits the context of showing a strong sense of responsibility.

12. 他对这次的失败 ____________ ，希望能从中吸取教训。





Correct answer: C 深感懊悔

“深感懊悔” means deeply regretful, which fits the context of feeling regretful about failure.

13. 他在 ____________ 面前表现得非常冷静。





Correct answer: A 困难

“困难” means difficulties, which fits the context of being calm in the face of difficulties.

14. 他对自己的 ____________ 感到非常自豪。





Correct answer: B 成就

“成就” means achievements, which fits the context of feeling proud of one's achievements.

15. ____________ 他遇到了很多困难，但他从未放弃。





Correct answer: B 虽然

“虽然” means although, which fits the context of stating that despite difficulties, he never gave up.

16. 他在团队中 ____________ 了重要的作用。





Correct answer: A 起到了

“起到了” means to play (a role), which fits the context of playing an important role in the team.

17. 他在演讲中 ____________ 地回答了所有问题。





Correct answer: B 自信

“自信” means confident, which fits the context of answering questions confidently.

18. ____________ 天气很冷，但他依然坚持每天早起跑步。





Correct answer: C 虽然

“虽然” means although, which fits the context of stating that despite the cold weather, he still insists on running.

19. 他在 ____________ 中取得了显著的进步。





Correct answer: A 学习

“学习” means study, which fits the context of making significant progress in studies.

20. 他在 ____________ 中展示了出色的领导能力。





Correct answer: D 会议

“会议” means meeting, which fits the context of showing excellent leadership skills in meetings.
